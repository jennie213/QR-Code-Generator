FAQs about QR Codes

What is a QR Code?

QR codes are two-dimensional barcodes that store information, readable by smartphones.

How do QR Codes work and what are they used for?

They encode data into a pattern of black and white squares that can be scanned.
They can store URLs, contact details, and other information for quick access.

How secure are QR Codes?

QR codes themselves are not inherently secure but can link to secure sites.
